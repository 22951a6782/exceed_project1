Degree Levels Available: Associate, bachelors, masters, Ph.D. Popular Specializations: Hardware systems, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence and robotics, cyber-physical systems Common Career Paths: Computer hardware engineer, circuit designer, hardware systems designer, technical writer, web developer Major Overview: Computer engineering majors learn how to design, build and operate semiconductor hardware and software systems. Computer engineers research, design and develop various computer components, such as circuit boards, processors, memory devices and routers. They earn a median annual salary of $132,360. Most computer engineering positions require a bachelors degree.
